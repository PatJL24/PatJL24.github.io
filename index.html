About Me

I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented computer science graduate, having earned my Bachelor of Science with Honors in Computer Science from York University in October 2023.

One of the best experiences during my time at university is when I got to be a a Business Analyst Intern at goeasy LTD. as part of the co-op program at York University. I collaborated with stakeholders, documented high-level requirements, and analyzed business challenges, all while adapting to shifting priorities and tight deadlines. I excelled in creating functional and technical diagrams, documenting requirements, and coordinating with stakeholders for effective testing. Being a Business Analyst Intern provided me great insight on what and how a team functions to make a project sucessful and I plan to take those insights with me for my future endeavers.

My passion for software development shines through my diverse project portfolio. One noteworthy project, Crystal Optical, showcases my skills in Java, HTML, CSS, and TypeScript/JavaScript. I was apart of a group of four, developing of a web application for an online luxury glasses store, including intuitive UI/UX design and the integration of a chatbot for customer support. Additionally, my work on the Instrument App in Flutter created an immersive user experience for interacting with various instruments.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy spending time watching TV shows, and movies. However, I'm trying to spend less time in front of a monitor and more time being active, such as running or shadow boxing. I'm always trying to learn new things and getting new experiences so feel free to reach out!
